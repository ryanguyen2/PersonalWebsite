Panthers' Lair Entrepreneuship Competition

Mar 2022-

In Feburary of 2022, I entered the Panthers Lair Entrepreneuship Competition, run by Candore College. My partners and I came up with a service called FormFixer; an app that critically gives an in-depth analysis of an individuals form when working out. By tracking the user's body position, speed, and angles at which they perform the movement, they will recieve analytical data that will have the purpose of preventing injuries when exercising. Unfortunately, my group did not succeed in advancing to become one of the finalists, however, I was able to learn more about how to make a business plan, the financial aspect of a start up, and so on.
